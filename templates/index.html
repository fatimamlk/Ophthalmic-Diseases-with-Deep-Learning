Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our deep learning-based diabetic retinopathy detection tool and how it can help you manage your eye health effectively.

  • What is diabetic retinopathy?

    Diabetic retinopathy is a diabetes complication that affects the eyes. It is caused by damage to the blood vessels of the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye (retina). Early detection is crucial to prevent vision loss.

  • How does the deep learning model work?

    Our deep learning model analyzes retinal images to detect signs of diabetic retinopathy. It has been trained on a large dataset of retinal images and can accurately identify the presence and severity of the disease.

  • How accurate is the detection tool?

    Our tool has been validated with high accuracy, ensuring reliable detection of diabetic retinopathy. However, it should be used as a supplementary tool and not replace professional medical advice.

  • How can I use the detection tool?

    Simply upload a clear image of your retina taken by a medical professional. Our tool will analyze the image and provide a prediction on the presence of diabetic retinopathy.

  • What should I do if the tool detects diabetic retinopathy?

    If our tool detects signs of diabetic retinopathy, we recommend consulting with an eye care professional for a comprehensive examination and appropriate treatment.
